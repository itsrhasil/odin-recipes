Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical

lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other

ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps to follow:

  1. Preheat the oven. Set it to 375 F degrees
  2. Brown the beef:
    1. In a sauce pan or skillet, cook the ground meat until no longer pink.
    2. Add chopped onion, 1 tbsp of the oregano, salt, pepper and continue cooking until onion is cooked.
  3. Prepare the sauces:
    1. Add the marinara sauce to the skillet and the cup of water.
    2. Stir and bring to a boil. Remove from heat and set aside.
    3. In a small bowl combine the ricotta cheese, 1 tbsp oregano, eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt and pepper.
  4. Stack the lasagna:
    1. In a baking dish, start assembling your lasagna.
    2. Start with couple ladles of the sauce at the bottom, this is necessary now that the noodles aren't cooked.
    3. Lay 3 lasagna noodles over the meat sauce.
    4. Next layer with ricotta mixture and about 1/2 cup of the mozzarella cheese.
    5. Repeat until you have 4 layers of noodles, and end with ricotta cheese and mozzarella cheese.
